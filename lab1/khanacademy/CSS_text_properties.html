10 Interesting Facts About Cats

About cats

Cats are among the most mysterious and charming creatures, having accompanied humans for millennia.
They attract us with their independence, grace, and unpredictable behavior.
But these furry pets conceal a whole world of
amazing facts and characteristics that many people are unaware of.

Facts

1. Cats can distinguish around 100 different sounds, making them more communicative than dogs.
2. Cats have excellent night vision: they can see in low-light conditions six times better than humans
3. Unlike most mammals, cats cannot taste sweets due to the lack of appropriate taste buds.
4. Cats are known to have a phenomenal sense of balance, allowing them to land on their feet when falling from a height.
